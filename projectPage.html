Credit card approval prediction model
Jupyter Notebook | Python | Machine learning
A machine learning model is created to predict credit card approval likelihood, utilizing approximately 25 relevant variables. The process involves gathering, cleaning, and visualizing data, followed by training and evaluation through regression analysis. This streamlined approach ensures the model's ability to provide concise insights into the factors influencing credit card approval decisions.
Branch Service Evaluation
Power Bi | Excel | MS Forms | Data analyst and visualization
An automated solution for conducting manual and voice call surveys is implemented. Customer responses are gathered through Microsoft Forms, with QR codes distributed within branches for easy access to the survey links. The collected customer responses are recorded in Excel, and a comprehensive dashboard is created using Power BI. This dashboard serves to streamline decision-making processes by providing a visual representation of the survey data, enhancing efficiency and insights derived from customer feedback.
